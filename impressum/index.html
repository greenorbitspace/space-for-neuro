Empowering Neurodiversity in Space

Space for Neurodiversity advances inclusion, awareness, and meaningful participation of neurodivergent individuals in the space sector. We combine research, advocacy, and community engagement to create more inclusive workplaces, policies, and opportunities across the industry.
