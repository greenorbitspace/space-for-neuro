Advancing Neurodiversity Across the Space Sector

Space for Neurodiversity is a pioneering initiative promoting inclusion, representation, and meaningful participation of neurodivergent individuals in space-related fields. We partner with organisations, research institutions, and innovators to:

  • Create inclusive workplaces and practices for neurodivergent talent
  • Deliver educational resources, guidance, and frameworks for equity
  • Foster collaboration, awareness, and community engagement across the sector

We believe lasting impact requires vision, advocacy, and actionable insight, and we bring all three to every project, partnership, and initiative.

Inclusive Practices

We provide guidance and support to ensure organisations adopt policies and practices that empower neurodivergent team members to thrive.

Awareness & Advocacy

Through research, workshops, and public initiatives, we raise awareness about neurodiversity and drive systemic change in the space industry.

Our Approach

How Impact Orbit Group Delivers Value
Step 01

Discovery & Insight

We start by understanding your challenges, context, and goals across the space ecosystem. This includes sector trends, sustainability opportunities, digital maturity, stakeholder needs, and organisational realities. Insight drives clarity and identifies strategic levers for impact.

Step 02

Strategy & Design

Building on insights, we co-create strategies tailored to your objectives—whether that’s digital transformation via Green Orbit Digital, communications and PR campaigns through Green Orbit Space Communications & PR, capability development via Green Orbit Academy, or event-led engagement like Space Impact Forum and Leicester Space Week. Our outputs are structured, evidence-based, and actionable.

Step 03

Implementation & Collaboration

We translate strategy into action—developing campaigns, digital systems, communications frameworks, impact models, and events. Across all ventures, collaboration, agility, and measurable progress ensure your organisation achieves tangible outcomes and long-term impact.

Step 04

Review, Learn & Scale

We embed learning, optimise approaches, and scale solutions across your initiatives. From sustainability programmes to digital platforms and public engagement events, our focus is on resilience, continuous improvement, and creating lasting positive impact throughout the space ecosystem.
